# L1.1 — all scenarios are well-formed

**Test:** `test_L1_all_scenarios_wellformed_conform`
**Layer:** SHACL well-formedness · **Status:** GREEN (built)

## What it checks
Every one of the 12 supply-chain scenarios, emitted with full metadata, has
**no well-formedness violations** — no unit, metadata, role, or leaf-rule errors.
Conservation and coarse/fine violations are deliberately ignored here (those are
L2 / L5); this test isolates *structural* validity only.

## Checked against
The SHACL shapes in `composition-statement-shapes.ttl`. The set of "well-formedness"
messages is filtered by keyword (`per 1 kg`, `must have`, `must be`, `must record`,
`must point`, `leaf`, `individual`); the assertion is that NONE appear.

## Expected outcome
For all 12 scenarios: zero well-formedness violations.

## Why it matters
Proves the builder emits structurally valid data. If this fails, later test
failures would be ambiguous (malformed fixture vs. real behaviour). It is the
guard that keeps every other test honest.

None. Locks current shape behaviour.
